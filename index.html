    DelayStation

    Games controller software and interface for adventures in sampling and synthesis, developed in Max/MSP.

    The software exploits data from a Sony DS4's motion sensors, as well as capturing joystick gestures and button interaction. Sample, loop, trigger synths and control FX parameters to build experimental compositions.

    Labour Movements

    An interactive piece for performer, using live electronics and custom software developed in Max/MSP and openFrameworks (C++).

    Labour Movements explores the potentials for future bio-acoustical research into meaningful contact between humans and cosmic-subterranean rodents.

    Formant Synthesis

    A Max patch exploring simple filter bank formant synthesis. Concatenate vowel sounds to produce words and noises, in simulation of the human vocal tract.
